Sales Analysis of Fern N Petals (FNP)
Overview
The Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of FNP’s sales performance for the year 2023. It consolidates key metrics such as total orders, total revenue, delivery times, and customer spending patterns. The dashboard also highlights sales trends across occasions, product categories, months, hours of the day, and geographic locations.

Revenue by Occasion
•	Top Occasions: Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan generated the highest revenue, each contributing around ₹6–7 lakh.
•	Other Strong Performers: Holi and All Occasions sales also contributed significantly.
•	Underperformers: Birthday and Valentine’s Day showed relatively lower revenue, indicating potential for promotional focus during these events.
Actionable Insight:
Focus marketing campaigns and product bundles around Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan, while introducing targeted offers for Birthday and Valentine’s Day to balance performance.

Revenue by Category
•	Soft Toys: ₹18,273 (54% share) — The leading category by a significant margin.
•	Colors: ₹11,548 (34% share) — Strong secondary contributor.
•	Mugs: ₹4,257 (12% share) — Smallest share, potential for cross-selling opportunities.
Actionable Insight:
Expand inventory and promotional efforts for Soft Toys, while introducing themed designs or combos for Mugs to boost category diversity.
Revenue by Month
•	Highest Revenue Month: February (₹80,352), likely driven by Valentine’s Day promotions.
•	Other Highs: May and October also showed good performance (~₹60–70k).
•	Lowest Revenue Months: April and September saw noticeable dips.
Actionable Insight:
Strengthen off-season months (April, September) with festival previews, discounts, or loyalty programs.
Revenue by Hour (Order Time)
•	Sales peak around 6 AM and 6 PM, with smaller surges throughout the day.
•	This suggests a dual shopping pattern — early morning and evening online activity.
Actionable Insight:
Schedule promotional email campaigns and social ads during these peak hours for higher engagement and conversion.

Top 10 Cities by Orders
•	Leading City: Kalyan-Dombivli (7 orders)
•	Other Strong Cities: Tiruchirappalli, Kochi, Alwar, Bhavnagar, and Chinsurah.
•	Geographical Spread: Orders are diversified across Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities.
Actionable Insight:
Expand delivery coverage and marketing focus on Kalyan-Dombivli and southern markets like Kochi and Tiruchirappalli, as they demonstrate strong engagement potential.

Operational Insights:
•	Average Delivery Time (5.53 days) can be improved through logistics optimization or local fulfillment centers.
•	Customer engagement appears healthy, but retention can be improved with loyalty programs, faster deliveries, and personalized recommendations.

Strategic Recommendations:

1.	Product Strategy: Expand high-performing categories (Soft Toys, Gift Sets) and enhance variety in underperforming categories like Mugs.
2.	Marketing:
o	Target campaigns around top occasions and cities.
o	Introduce flash sales in low-performing months.
3.	Operational: Optimize logistics to reduce average delivery time below 4 days.
4.	Customer Experience: Launch referral or reward programs to boost repeat purchases.

Conclusion
The sales data reveals a stable and opportunity-rich performance for FNP in 2023. The business shows strong sales across specific categories and occasions, but there is potential to expand revenue through better regional targeting, product diversification, and delivery efficiency.
